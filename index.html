What this is

Film for people who make films.

The Homegrown Short Film Showcase isn't a competition. There are no judges, no ranked selections, no gatekeepers deciding whose story deserves to be seen. It's a community screening — nights of short films from Alberta filmmakers at every stage, followed by an Industry Night where the people making things meet the people who can help them make more.

Year Three spans Red Deer and Calgary for the first time, with a dedicated opening night for Indigenous filmmakers in Red Deer — because the first step to building an Alberta film community is actually showing up.

For filmmakers

Your film belongs here.

We're looking for short films made by Alberta-connected filmmakers — first-timers, students, and working professionals alike. The work doesn't have to be polished. It has to be honest.

Eligibility
Short films from Alberta filmmakers. All genres. All experience levels. First submissions welcome.
Format
Submit via FilmFreeway or Zeffy. Screeners via Vimeo, YouTube, or Drive.
Deadline
Submissions open now. Early deadline TBA. Check back for updates.
What we look for
Storytelling over polish. Specificity over spectacle. Community over credentials.
Fee waivers are available. Email community@wearehomegrown.ca — no explanation required.
